## Executive Profile

I am a cybersecurity practitioner focused on defensive operations, log analysis, vulnerability analysis, and system behavior. Through the Merit America Cybersecurity Program and independent work through GLITCH, I am building hands-on capability in incident response, telemetry-driven investigation, and structured reporting.

My approach emphasizes interpretation over output — understanding not just what tools report, but what findings mean, why they matter, and how they should be addressed.

All research and testing is conducted in isolated, authorized lab environments.
