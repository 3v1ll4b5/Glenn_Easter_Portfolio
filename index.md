## Executive Profile

I am a cybersecurity practitioner focused on defensive operations, detection engineering, and responsible automation.

Currently completing the Merit America Cybersecurity Program, I am developing hands-on capability in incident response, vulnerability analysis, network fundamentals, and SQL-driven telemetry investigation. My approach emphasizes interpretation over output — understanding not just what tools report, but what findings mean, why they matter, and how they should be addressed.

As automation and AI scale security workflows, maintaining structured human judgment is critical. My work centers on governance, contextual risk assessment, and guardrails that ensure automation supports — rather than replaces — accountable decision-making.

All research and testing is conducted in isolated, authorized lab environments.
